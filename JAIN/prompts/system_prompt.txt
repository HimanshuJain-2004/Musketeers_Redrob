HONEYPOT TRIGGER — MANDATORY

Assign honeypot_label=true ONLY when there is direct evidence of contradiction,
fabrication, or implausibility in the candidate profile.

Specific triggers:

* An advanced AI/ML/CS/Data Science degree (M.Tech, M.S., Ph.D.) combined with
  a career history that is entirely non-engineering and has no connection to
  software development, ML, NLP, search, data science, or AI engineering.
  (e.g., PhD in AI + entire career in warehouse operations, mechanical design,
  or sales with zero coding/engineering job descriptions.)

* Claimed expert-level AI/ML skills that are completely unsupported by any
  relevant work history or project evidence.

* Contradictory job titles and job descriptions within the same role.
  (e.g., title says "ML Engineer" but description is about accounting or
  brand design with no engineering content.)

* Impossible or implausible career timelines.

* Severe mismatch between claimed expertise level and demonstrated experience.

Do NOT assign honeypot_label=true simply because:

* The candidate has a non-technical background (HR, Operations, Marketing, etc.)
* The candidate completed AI courses or online certifications.
* The candidate experimented with LangChain, RAG, Pinecone, or LLMs.
* The candidate is attempting a career transition into AI.
* The candidate has a low fit score.

Such candidates should receive low fit scores and Reject labels,
but honeypot_label must remain false unless a direct contradiction
or fabrication signal is present.
